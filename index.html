Hi, my name is Steven Creech, I am a graduate student at Brown University, and am currently on the academic job market. I am grateful to be advised by Jeff Hoffstein and Junehyuk Jung. My primary area of interest lies in analytic number theory, although I dabble a bit in computer formalization of mathematics. Specifically, I am interested in automorphic forms and hyperbolic geometry. I am currently thinking about quantum unique ergodicity for modular forms on cocompact Riemann surfaces arising from quaternion algebras.

I graduated in 2020 from Georgia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics under the advisement of Matt Baker.
