Hi, my name is Steven Creech, I am a graduate student at Brown University under the advisement of Jeff Hoffstein and Junehyuk Jung. My primary area of interest lies in number theory. Specifically, I am interested in automorphic forms and hyperbolic geometry. I am currently thinking about quantum unique ergodicity for modular forms on cocompact Riemann surfaces arising from quaternion algebras. I graduated in 2020 from Georgia Tech with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics under the advisement of Matt Baker.
